The name started as a joke. People always asked what's the tea, expecting gossip. I never had gossip. I had perspective. So I started serving it intentionally.

That's still the throughline.

## What This Is

The Intentional Tea is a translation and systems brand. Every product, service, and piece of writing here does the same thing: takes what is complex, overwhelming, or unexamined and converts it into something you can actually live by.

The digital planners give you structure without rigidity. The Life Reset gives you a process for the parts of your life that feel stuck. The Astrology Reports give you language for patterns you already sense but can't articulate. The Work With Me services build the backend infrastructure so your business runs without you manually doing everything.

Same skill set. Different application.

## What This Isn't

This isn't a wellness brand or a productivity brand or a self-help brand. Those exist and some of them are good.

This is for the person who already knows what they want and needs architecture to get there. If you're still figuring out what you want, start with the Life Reset. That's what it's built for.

## What to Expect Here

The blog is where I think out loud. Strategy, perspective, and the occasional reframe. I write about time, money, energy, and the systems that connect all three. Not abstract theory — specific, usable thinking.

Posts come when they're ready. No content calendar, no manufactured consistency. Quality over cadence.

Welcome to the ecosystem.

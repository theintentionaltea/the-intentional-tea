About

Architecture for an Intentional Life

My story

After six years in corporate operations and sales, building infrastructure for global tech companies, I stopped building other people's systems and started building my own. The Intentional Tea is that build. A brand, a product line, and a digital ecosystem I architected from scratch.

The name started as a joke. People always asked what's the tea, expecting gossip. I never had gossip. I had perspective. So I started serving it intentionally. That's still the throughline.

The Work With Me services are the same work, done for others. If you're a small business owner with a real product and a chaotic backend, I come in and build the infrastructure that makes it run. Same skill set. Different direction.

— Tiara

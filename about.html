My Story

I've always been the friend who sends the product links, gives the unsolicited TED Talk, and has a framework for whatever you're going through. Before The Intentional Tea existed, I spent years working at the intersection of sales, finance, operations, and instructional design at one of the largest tech companies in the world, building systems and training infrastructure that I genuinely loved making, inside an environment that was slowly making it impossible to stay.

When the layoff came, I took it as the exit I hadn't let myself take yet (even though the timing could not have been more inconvenient). What came out of that time was the realization that I didn't have to choose between the analytical side of my brain and the creative one, between structure and softness, between building for a corporation and building for myself. I just had to point everything I already knew in a different direction.

"People always asked what's the tea. I never had gossip. I had perspective."

The Intentional Tea is a two-part brand. One side is centered on intentional living, from digital planning tools designed for the way your brain actually works to personalized astrology and human design reports that give you real architecture for your life. The other side is Business Elevation Services, where I bring that same systems-and-infrastructure skill set to small business owners who need their backend to actually function.

The name started as a joke. People always asked what's the tea, expecting gossip. I never had gossip. I had perspective. So I started serving it intentionally, and that's still the throughline for everything here.

Meet Tiara

My story

For six years in corporate, I held a role that didn't fit neatly into one department. The work sat at the intersection of sales, finance, operations, training, and learning design, because doing it well required someone who could speak fluently to all of them at once. I was the connective layer. The person who understood the product, could train teams on it, audit the process around it, and flag when something wasn't working operationally.

I didn't have a single title that covered all of that. I had a skill set that crossed every title.

When I left corporate, I didn't leave those skills. I redirected them. The Intentional Tea is what happened when I applied the same operational discipline I'd spent years building inside someone else's infrastructure to my own life, my own business, and my own design.

The name started as a joke. People would always ask me "what's the tea?" expecting gossip. I never had gossip. I had perspective. So instead of spilling tea, I started serving it intentionally. That became the throughline. I don't just share information. I translate it into something usable, whether that's a planner, a reset guide, or a personalized cosmic report.

— Tiara

The Foundation

Built on the Same Skill Set

The Intentional Tea is not a planner brand or a wellness brand. It's a translation and systems brand. The through line across every product is one consistent action: take what is complex, overwhelming, or unexamined and convert it into something you can actually live by.

The digital planning tools do that for your time and daily structure. The Work With Me services do that for your business backend. The Intentional Astrology Report does that for how you understand your own design. Different formats, same foundation.

Three pillars run through all of it: Time, Security, and Energy. Sustainable infrastructure, whether for a life or a business, comes down to those three things.

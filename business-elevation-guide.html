02 Hello & Welcome The Intentional Tea
Hello & Welcome
I'm Tiara —
and your business
deserves infrastructure
that actually holds.

I spent six years in corporate operations, sales enablement, and instructional design, building the systems that kept other people's companies running. Then I left, built my own digital business from scratch — products, content systems, automation workflows, multi-platform strategy — and figured out pretty quickly what the difference is between a business that looks operational and one that actually is.

The gap is almost never the product. Small businesses that reach out to me already have the thing — a real service, real customers, real years of experience. What they're missing is the structure around it: consistent pricing, a professional web presence, booking systems that don't require a text to every customer, and documentation that makes the whole thing run without the owner being the connective tissue for everything.

That's what Business Elevation is. I come in, do the research they'd never do themselves, build the infrastructure, and hand them something that runs. They keep doing what they're good at. The backend handles itself.

In this guide

You'll find a breakdown of the methodology, what's included in each engagement, what to expect from the process, and the details you need to decide what's right for your business.

This is for established businesses with a real product and real customers — not businesses still finding their footing. The operational chaos comes from not having professional infrastructure, not from lacking a viable offer.

  • Customers reach out but your booking process is chaos — texts, DMs, verbal confirmations, none of it connected
  • Your pricing is inconsistent, or you've been quoting from memory and you're not sure your numbers are right anymore
  • You have no website, or the one you have doesn't reflect where you actually are or what you actually charge
  • You're doing manually what should be automated — confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, payment collection
  • You've been meaning to "get organized" for a while, but the day-to-day keeps coming and it never happens
  • Someone handed you a referral and you want to look and function like the business the referral implied you were
This is not for

Businesses just starting out that need help finding their product or audience. Business Elevation is for businesses that already have the product, the customers, and the track record — and need someone to build the professional infrastructure around what's already working.

04 The Process The Intentional Tea
The Methodology
Five phases.
One complete
operational build.

Every engagement follows the same five-phase structure. The depth and deliverables scale by tier — but the methodology is consistent regardless of which package you choose.

01
Discovery
An independent audit of everything that already exists — Google Drive, existing forms, social accounts, whatever you have. I review it before asking questions, so when we talk, we're not starting from zero.
02
Research & Analysis
Four to six direct competitors researched. Pricing history reconstructed. Market rates mapped. Tool audit completed. You would never spend eight hours doing this. I do it once, document it cleanly, and hand you conclusions that would have taken months to arrive at.
03
The Business Elevation Report
Your signature deliverable. A complete professional document covering competitive landscape, unified pricing recommendation, add-on and upsell strategy, operations framework, website plan, and tech stack. It's yours whether you continue or not — though most clients do.
04
Build
Website, booking system, automations, Google Business Profile, intake forms, CRM, and all supporting documents. Nothing in the report stays hypothetical. It gets built.
05
Handoff & Training
Complete handoff document. Recorded 60-minute training call. How-to guides for the tools you'll use most. 30-day check-in to make sure everything is running correctly.

09 Frequently Asked Questions The Intentional Tea
Frequently Asked Questions
Questions you
probably have.
Do I need to have a certain size business or revenue level?
No. The Digital Presence tier is specifically designed for businesses in early operational stages. What matters is that you have a real product or service and real customers — not a particular revenue threshold.
What if I'm not sure which tier is right for me?
Fill out the inquiry form. After reviewing your responses, I'll tell you which tier makes sense and why. You don't have to figure that out before you reach out.
Do you do discovery calls before I pay?
No. The inquiry form is the first step. After you submit, I respond within 48 hours with a recommendation. If you want to move forward, you pay the $350 deposit. The kickoff call is the start of the work, not a sales conversation.
What if the business owner isn't comfortable with new tools?
That's exactly who I build for. The tools I implement are ones your team can actually use. The handoff documentation and training call are designed for non-technical owners.
What industries do you work with?
Small service businesses where the owner delivers the service directly and the chaos comes from not having professional infrastructure. Event services, personal services, food and beverage, health and wellness, creative services, trades. If you're wondering whether you fit, fill out the form and I'll tell you directly.
Can I add ongoing support after the project is done?
Yes. Monthly or quarterly, added at any time after handoff. Priced at $100/$150/$250/month by tier, with 17% off quarterly. A $150 re-onboarding fee applies if more than 60 days have passed since project close.
